#####如何安裝模組包#####

0.執行乾淨1.7.10版本
1.安裝forge-1.7.10-10.13.4.1614-1.7.10-installer-win.exe
2.搜尋並輸入%appdata%
3.進入.minecraft
4.進入mods資料夾
5.如果資料夾不存在，創建一個在.minecraft
6.將壓縮包中裡的jar檔案放進mods資料夾
7.玩


#####How to install Mods#####

0.Play Minecraft 1.7.10
1.install forge-1.7.10-10.13.4.1614-1.7.10-installer-win.exe
2.Go to %appdata%.
3.Go to .minecraft
4.Go to mods folder.
5.If the “mods” folder does not exist you can create one to .minecraft
6.Drag and drop the mod jar file into it.
7.Play
